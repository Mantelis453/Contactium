## Getting Started

### What is Contactium?

Contactium is an AI-powered cold email outreach platform designed to help you:
- Connect with potential clients efficiently
- Generate personalized email campaigns using AI
- Track campaign performance and engagement
- Manage contacts and companies in one place

## Creating Campaigns

### Campaign Creation Workflow

#### Step 1: Campaign Details

**Campaign Name**
- Use descriptive names (e.g., "Tech Startups Q1 2025")
- Helps organize and track multiple campaigns
- Can include dates, industries, or objectives

**Subject Line**
- Keep under 60 characters for best results
- Personalize with recipient information
- Avoid spam trigger words (FREE, URGENT, etc.)
- Test different subject lines for A/B testing

#### Step 2: Email Content

**Manual Mode**
- Write your own email template
- Use personalization variables
- Full control over messaging and tone

**AI Mode**
- Provide a brief description of your offering
- AI generates personalized email content
- Review and edit before approving
- Regenerate if needed for different variations

#### Step 3: Personalization Variables

Use these variables in your email templates:

```
{company_name}     - Recipient's company name
{recipient_name}   - Recipient's full name
{your_name}        - Your name from settings
{your_company}     - Your company name
```

**Example:**
```
Hi {recipient_name},

I noticed that {company_name} is growing rapidly in the tech sector...

Best regards,
{your_name}
{your_company}
```

#### Step 4: Target Audience

**Select Companies**
- Choose from your Companies database
- Filter by industry, size, or location
- Select individual companies or bulk select

**Select Contact List**
- Use pre-organized contact lists
- Available on Starter and Professional plans
- Ideal for segmented campaigns

#### Step 5: Schedule & Launch

**Send Immediately**
- Emails begin sending right away
- Respects daily sending limits
- Best for time-sensitive campaigns

**Schedule for Later**
- Set a specific date and time
- Plan campaigns in advance
- Optimal send times: Tue-Thu, 8-10 AM or 4-6 PM

**Save as Draft**
- Return to edit later
- Perfect for collaborative review
- No emails sent until you launch

### AI Email Generation

#### How AI Works

1. You provide campaign context and goals
2. AI analyzes successful email patterns
3. Generates personalized, professional content
4. Creates compelling subject lines and CTAs

#### Tips for Better AI Results

- **Be specific**: Describe your offering in detail
- **Include value proposition**: What problem do you solve?
- **Mention target industry**: Helps AI personalize tone
- **State desired outcome**: Meeting, demo, call, etc.

#### Example AI Prompts

**Good:**
```
We offer AI-powered analytics software for e-commerce businesses.
Helps reduce cart abandonment by 30%. Target: online retailers
with 100+ daily orders. Goal: Schedule 15-minute demo.
```

**Poor:**
```
Our product is great and everyone should use it.
```

## Managing Contact Lists

*Available on Starter and Professional plans*

### What are Contact Lists?

Contact Lists help you organize prospects into targeted groups for more effective campaigns.

### Creating a Contact List

1. Navigate to **Contact Lists** in the main menu
2. Click **+ New List**
3. Enter a descriptive name (e.g., "Tech Startups", "Warm Leads")
4. Add optional description
5. Click **Create List**

### Adding Contacts

**Individual Contact**
1. Open your contact list
2. Click **+ Add Contact**
3. Fill in required information:
   - Email address (required)
   - Name
   - Company
   - Notes
4. Click **Add Contact**

**Bulk Import** (Coming Soon)
- CSV file upload
- Automatic field mapping
- Duplicate detection

### Organizing Contacts

**Segmentation Strategies:**

- **By Industry**: Tech, Healthcare, Finance, etc.
- **By Company Size**: Startups, SMB, Enterprise
- **By Stage**: Cold, Warm, Hot leads
- **By Location**: City, region, country
- **By Engagement**: Opened, Replied, Converted

### Using Contact Lists in Campaigns

When creating a campaign:
1. Select "Use Contact List" as your target audience
2. Choose one or more contact lists
3. Review contact count
4. Launch campaign

## Companies Database

### Building Your Database

The Companies database stores information about target businesses for your campaigns.

### Adding Companies

1. Go to **Companies** in the main menu
2. Click **+ Add Company**
3. Enter company information:
   - Company Name (required)
   - Website URL
   - Industry
   - Contact Email
   - Description/Notes
4. Click **Add Company**

### Company Information Fields

**Company Name**
- Official business name
- Used in email personalization
- Searchable in campaigns

**Website**
- Company website URL
- Helps verify legitimacy
- Reference for research

**Industry**
- Business sector or category
- Used for filtering and targeting
- Examples: Technology, Healthcare, Finance

**Contact Email**
- Primary contact or general inquiry email
- Used as campaign recipient
- Should be verified before campaigns

**Description/Notes**
- Internal notes about the company
- Why they're a good fit
- Previous interactions
- Research findings

### Managing Companies

**Search**
- Find companies quickly by name
- Filter by industry or criteria
- Sort by date added

**Edit**
- Update company information anytime
- Keep records current
- Add new insights

**Delete**
- Remove companies you no longer target
- Permanently removes from database
- Warning: Cannot be undone

### Using Companies in Campaigns

When creating a campaign:
1. Select "Use Companies Database"
2. Filter by industry, size, or location (if available)
3. Select individual companies or bulk select
4. Review selection before launching

## Best Practices

### Email Deliverability

#### Warming Up Your Domain

**Why It Matters:**
- New sending domains can be flagged as spam
- Build reputation gradually
- Improves inbox placement

**How to Warm Up:**
1. **Week 1**: Send 10-20 emails per day
2. **Week 2**: Send 20-40 emails per day
3. **Week 3**: Send 40-80 emails per day
4. **Week 4+**: Gradually increase to target volume

#### Avoiding Spam Filters

**Do:**
- ‚úÖ Personalize every email
- ‚úÖ Use recipient's name and company
- ‚úÖ Keep subject lines natural and relevant
- ‚úÖ Include physical business address
- ‚úÖ Provide easy unsubscribe option
- ‚úÖ Send from a professional domain
- ‚úÖ Maintain clean email lists

**Don't:**
- ‚ùå Use ALL CAPS in subject lines
- ‚ùå Overuse exclamation marks!!!
- ‚ùå Include misleading subject lines
- ‚ùå Send to unverified email addresses
- ‚ùå Use spam trigger words (FREE, URGENT, ACT NOW)
- ‚ùå Send identical emails to everyone
- ‚ùå Ignore bounce and unsubscribe requests

### Writing Effective Cold Emails

#### Structure Template

```
Subject: [Personalized, relevant, under 60 characters]

Hi [Recipient Name],

[Opening: Show you've done research]
[1-2 sentences about them or their company]

[Value Proposition: What problem do you solve?]
[2-3 sentences on how you help companies like theirs]

[Social Proof: Brief credibility statement]
[1 sentence: clients, results, credentials]

[Call-to-Action: Specific, low-commitment ask]
[1 sentence asking for meeting, call, or reply]

Best regards,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Company]
[Contact Information]
```
